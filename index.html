Meet Dr. Gaurav Mathpal

B.D.S., M.D.S. (Prosthodontics & Implantology)

"My philosophy is simple: Treat every patient with the same level of care and precision I would expect for my own family."

We are delighted to introduce you to our esteemed dental practice, helmed by the eminent Dr. Gaurav Mathpal, a distinguished specialist in the fields of Prosthodontics and Implantology.

Dr. Mathpal is a highly qualified professional, having earned both a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S) and a Master of Dental Surgery (M.D.S). With a wealth of experience spanning over a decade, his expertise and commitment to excellence make him a sought-after practitioner in the dental community.

At Dr. Gaurav Mathpal's clinic, we understand that dental health is integral to overall well-being. As such, we offer comprehensive root canal treatments to address any discomfort while safeguarding the integrity of your natural teeth. Additionally, we specialize in managing Temporo-Mandibular Joint Disorders, ensuring optimal functionality and comfort in your daily life.
