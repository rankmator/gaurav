Patient Education

Frequently Asked Questions About Smile Designing

Comprehensive clinical answers to address your cosmetic dentistry inquiries.

What is Digital Smile Design (DSD) and how does it work?

Digital Smile Design (DSD) is an advanced aesthetic planning protocol where high-definition facial photography, 3D intraoral scans, and dynamic video analysis are combined. This allows Dr. Gaurav Mathpal to digitally craft your bespoke smile in harmony with your facial proportions, lips, and eye lines before any procedure begins.

Do porcelain veneers require grinding down healthy natural teeth?

In modern aesthetic prosthodontics, Dr. Mathpal employs ultra-thin (0.3mm) E.max laminate veneers and minimal-prep or zero-prep protocols. Micro-enamel reshaping is restricted to a fraction of a millimeter, preserving the structural strength of your natural tooth while delivering superior fluorescence and shade.

How long do custom E.max porcelain veneers last?

Medical-grade lithium disilicate E.max ceramic veneers are non-porous, highly stain-resistant, and chemically bonded to natural tooth enamel. With routine oral hygiene and regular dental checkups, they retain their brilliant luster and structural integrity for 15 to 20+ years.

Can smile designing fix gummy smiles or uneven gum margins?

Yes. A complete smile makeover integrates precision diode laser gingival contouring (pink aesthetics) with ceramic veneers (white esthetics). This painlessly reshapes excess gum tissue, creates symmetric zeniths, and achieves a harmonious gum-to-tooth ratio.
